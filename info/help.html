Add Room:

  • In the Rooms view, tap the + in the upper-right.
  • In the resulting 'Add Room' view, type in the new Room name.
  • Tap the Save button and you're done.

Delete Room:

  • In the Rooms view, swipe left on the Room you want to delete.
  • A red Delete button will appear:
  • Tap Delete to delete the Room, tap anywhere else to cancel.

Add Item:

  • Tap the Room that contains the Item you want to add.
  • In the Items view, tap the + in the upper-right.
  • In the resulting Item Detail view, fill in the appropriate information. The data is saved as soon as you exit each text field so no save action is necessary.

Add Item Photo:

  • Go to the Item detail view for the Item you want to add photos to.
  • If this Item does not have any photos for it, tap the square in the upper-left that says 'Click to set photo'
  • If this Item already has a photo(s) attached to it, tap the photo in the upper-left.
  • This takes you to the Photos collection view.
  • Tap the camera button in the upper-right.
  • Using the camera interface, take a picture of the item.
  • Note: The last photo you take will be used in the Items list view.

Delete Item Photo:

  • Go to the Item detail view for the Item you want to add photos to.
  • Tap the photo in the upper-left.
  • Tap the photo that you want to delete.
  • Tap the delete (trash) icon in the lower-right to delete the photo.
  • This takes you to the Photos collection view.

Delete Item:

  • In the Items view, swipe left on the Item you want to delete.
  • A red Delete button will appear.
  • Tap Delete to delete the Item, tap anywhere else to cancel.

Edit Item:

  • Navigate to the room that contains the item, then tap the accessory detail.
  • Edit the field(s) as necessary.
  • Tap 'done' when you are satisfied with the edits.

Start Claim:

  • From the main Rooms view, tap the Claim button.
  • You will see a list of all your Items, sorted by Room. Tap each item that you lost, a checkmark will appear on the right.
  • When the list is to your satisfaction, tap the action button.
  • This will take you to the Claim Information view
  • You can enter Claim Information which will be used for the header of the report. This information is optional.
  • After you have entered the information, tap Save.
  • You can either Email or Print your claim
