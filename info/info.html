About HouseCat:

HouseCat is used to maintain an inventory of your valuables to be used in the case of a loss. If you suffer a loss, it will enable you to quickly provide a report to your insurance agent detailing the items lost, as well as their estimated value.

To get the best use out of this app, make sure to enter the information for your valuables as soon as you purchase them.

In the event of a loss, simply click on the button to generate a claim report. You can then either print the report out or email it directly to your insurance agent.

Where did the name come from? 'HouseCat' is a concatenation of House and Catalog.

Hints:

Before leaving for a vacation, add a room titled "vacation data" (replace date with the actual date of your vacation, or some other unique identifier).

As you pack, take pictures of your valuables.
